• Fix: Boss sessions now correctly deduct arrows from your inventory on completion, matching the behaviour of dungeon sessions
• Fix: XP bonus display when both a 2x boost and tithe blessing are active now shows the full multiplier chain (e.g. "232 x 2 x 1.25") instead of a misleading "(+116)" annotation

• Fix: XP estimates in the Skills sheet for Mining, Woodcutting, Fishing, and Agility now correctly factor in the active 2x XP boost and prayer blessing multiplier
