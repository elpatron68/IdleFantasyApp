# Titles

Titles are permanent, earned once and kept forever — even if the way you earned them later changes or is removed from the game. Equip one from the character edit sheet on the **Profile** screen (tap the pencil icon next to your name); your name then displays as "*Name* the *Title*" everywhere it normally appears. You can also see every title you haven't unlocked yet in the same picker, along with what's needed to earn it.

## Skill Mastery

Awarded for completing every quest in a skill's full quest chain. Combat, Agility, and Farming have no quest chain of their own, so they don't have a skill mastery title — see Guild Mastery below for those two instead.

## Seasonal

Completing a Seasonal Event awards a title unique to that event — for example, finishing the *Sunspire Solstice* event earns "the Champion of Sunspire Solstice." A new title is added for every Seasonal Event as it's released, and previously-earned seasonal titles are kept permanently even after the event ends.
